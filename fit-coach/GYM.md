## Progressive Overload Protocol

<!-- Choose your method and stick to it -->

**Method:** Double progression

1. Start at the low end of the rep range (e.g. 4×5)
2. Each week +1 rep per set until hitting the top of the range (e.g. 4×8)
3. Once top is hit → increase weight (+2.5kg upper / +5kg lower) and reset to low end
4. Accessories → focus on contraction quality over weight

**RIR control as secondary layer:**
- If top of range hit with high RIR (≥3) → increase load directly
- If can't reach bottom of range → don't increase; investigate cause (sleep, nutrition, stress, fatigue)

## Stagnation Management Notes

Strategies available in order of application:
1. **Micro-loads:** 1.25kg / 0.5kg increases
2. **Rep manipulation:** if stalling at 4×8, try 5×6 and progress reps
3. **Pause and tempo:** paused reps at bottom or 3s eccentric
4. **Programmed deload:** week at 60% volume and intensity (every 4-6 weeks, same exercises)
5. **Variant swap:** only after exhausting the above for 2-3 mesocycles
